Scrambled Eggs

Description

Really tasty eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 slice cheese
  • olive oil

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Coat pan with olive oil

    2. Step 2

  2. Crack eggs into bowl and break the yolks

    3. Step 3

  3. Shred Cheese and add to bowl with eggs

    4. Step 4

  4. Cook eggs on high while stiring until eggs are fully cooked
