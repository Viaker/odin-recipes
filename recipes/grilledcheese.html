Grilled cheese

Description

This is how to make perfect grilled cheee

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of Bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Preheat pan to med-high and butter your bread

    2. Step 2

  2. Put 2 slices of cheese overlapping in middle on non-buttered side of bread

    3. Step 3

  3. Put bread butter side down so cheese is on top and cover cheese with second bread(non butter side)

    4. Step 4

  4. cook until it slides around easy flip and repeat. When both sides are toasted to your preferance your done
